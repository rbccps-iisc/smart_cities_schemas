Schema Name: generic_iotdevice_schema

Base schemas: isco_base_schema

Target devices: Generic IoT devices which have a fixed, known location (For example, streetlights, 
Sensors mounted on buildings/utility poles etc.)

General description: 

Field descriptions:

Mandatory fields:

All mandatory fields from the base schema are applicable.


Additional mandatory fields:
"latitute"/"longitude": 
Lat/Long values. Although not mandatory we have provided a field to also
provide ontological references of what lat/long reference system has been
used to provide the lat/long values.

Other fields:
"altitude"
"geoLocation":
To provide information about device locations (e.g., mapid, indoor locations, address etc.)
